About US

We are family run small restaurant that has been around for over 30 years. Our Mom, Somphop Vichenker is the Chef since day 1 in the summer of 1986. Her passion of cooking has made our food to become one of the best Thai food in Montreal. We were rated as No. 1 for couple of times and we are still listed as one of the best Thai food especially our Pad Thai by few local magazines and blogs, such as Mirror, MtlBlog, Thrillist, etc.

We started this small business in Le Faubourg St. Catherine. It was one of the gem in the area. With the closure of the food court, our restaurant has now become a sitting-down restaurant in the Village. A very strategic place on St. Catherine E. that is steps away from Beaudry Metro.

We are Committed to use the finest ingredients in our recipes, our day usually start at 4am, go to the market and then prepare the food for the day. No food leaves our kitchen that we ourselves would not eat.
