CUISINE BANGKOK

AUTHENTIC THAI CUISINE

Like a phoenix rising from the noodles, popular cheap-eats Thai spot Cuisine Bangkok is back in business. Bangkok was pushed out of the Faubourg Sainte-Catherine shopping centre downtown along with a few other businesses in late 2016, but has since regrouped and found itself a new location right near Beaudry métro in the Village. It’s somewhat of an upgrade, they now have a storefront and a bigger space than the previous food court counter. But it’s too far for Concordia-adjacent students and workers to grab a quick lunch.
